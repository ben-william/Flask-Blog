About Me

Based in Austin!

I'm working on becoming a fullstack developer. My first experience with coding was using Matlab in college years ago. I excelled in the class and went on to tutor it in subsequent semesters. Then came a long hiatus until 2022.

In my current position, I realized learning to code could provide our startup significant leverage to scale our business. I started with some webscraping and really enjoyed those projects. It also proved to be a successful effort - we generated 100s of web pages within a matter of weeks that boosted traffic and income more than 10%!

I'm now fully committed to building a career in development. While I have a little JS experience, most of my work so far has been in Python. Because Python is so easy and quick to implement, my current plan is to explore as many concepts as I can before returning to JS. Once I am comfortable with vanilla JS, I aim to become proficient with Node, React, and Angular.

I am passionately determined to build my own webapps. I have been mapping out ideas as they come up so I can build and deploy them once my skillset is sufficient. If you're reading this and considering bringing me in for an interview, don't forget to ask me about them!
